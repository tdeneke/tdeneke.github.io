My interests lie at the intersection between computing and data-intensive applications. I explore ways of efficiently utilizing computing platforms with specialized hardware like GPUs for deep learning, video processing, and big data analytics. Lately, I have been working with several researchers on their computing environment needs, designing, developing, and assisting them on how to efficiently utilize HPC and Cloud platforms with specialized hardware like GPUs. I have also been involved in European projects related to federated machine learning and workflow engines 1 2.

Previously, I was working on efficient causality network discovery and predictive modeling approaches for large-scale time-series data with a focus on manufacturing processes 3. I have also done extensive research in understanding and modeling the workload of modern multimedia processing applications on various computing platforms to design a proactive computing resource management algorithm 4.
